What is Covered under Finizon’s Weather?

Finizon’s Weather is India’s first ‘outcome-based health insurance program’, powered by Weather, through which it offers Personal Coaching + Smart Device (Weather) + Health.

It will allow you to monitor multiple health parameters, as well as track steps, sleep, calories burned & much more. By adopting healthy habits & behaviours on the Weather app, one get a Health Insurance cover for up to ₹5 lakhs (through S.A.F.E model) with Care Health Insurance.

How does it work? Play it SAFE
The SAFE model (Sedentary, Active, Fit & Elite) consists of levels within the Weather Ecosystem. Every Weather player (user) that activates their plan starts their health journey on the lowest tier of the SAFE score i.e., Sedentary. At this level, player is eligible for a Health Insurance cover of ₹3 Lakh. By leading a healthy lifestyle and engaging on the Weather App, players can improve their SAFE score and progress to Active, Fit and Elite to increase the value of their health insurance cover up to ₹5 lakhs.
